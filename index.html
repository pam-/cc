about

Join the Vox Product Diversity and Inclusion Committee, on Nov 5th and 6th 2016 for Color Coded: an unhackathon that aims to empower Creatives of Color through collaboration, creativity, and self-care

Color Coded is an event created primarily for Creatives of Color. Why? Because we believe that true inclusivity begins with carving safe spaces for underrepresented communities. It isn't a competion. Instead, it is an occasion for participants to come together, bond through creativity, and rid themselves of stress.

Come surround yourself with positive energy, and get ready to work on a project that will benefit your community! An example of project is tonr, an app built during the Vox Product team yearly hackathon.

We are welcoming of all ideas, so don’t feel obligated to deliver software. Maybe you’d like to create a comic, a documentary, or even build a physical prototype? It’s your time to explore.

By attending, you agree to follow our Code of Conduct.

FAQ

Participants

+

Who can participate?

Do you consider yourself to be a creative? Do you have tons of app ideas you want to contribute for your community? Are you a Person of Color? If yes, you should participate!

I do not identify as a Person of Color, but I fully support the cause. Can I participate?

We welcome all applicants – but please keep in mind that this space is primarily created for people of color.

What does participating entail?

To participate, you have to be here for the entirety of the event. This includes both Saturday and Sunday. It will be a weekend filled with creative expression, connectivity, and positive energies!

Project Ideas

+

Is there a theme for ideas?

We encourage participants to think of ideas that benefit the Community of Color. An example of project is tonr, an app built during the Vox Product team yearly hackathon.

I have an idea! How will I share it?

Come prepared to pitch it Saturday morning! We will take an hour to allow all ideas to be shared.

I don't have an idea. Can I still participate?

Absolutely! There will be a pitching session on Saturday morning, as well as a pre-made list of project ideas.

Product

+

What do we expect you to create?

We expect participants to create a prototype of their ideas. Each prototype will be showcased at a science fair where all participants will be able to check out each other's ideas.

Will there be a winner?

Nope!
Each project will be showcased "science-fair" style. Participants, coaches, and possibly other attendees, will browse the room and check all the projects.
This is a judgment free zone.
